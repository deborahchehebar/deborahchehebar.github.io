    About

    I received my B.A. from N.Y.U.  with a major in Art History. I completed several architectural courses including field studies in New York City and Mexico City. I began decorating as a favor for family & friends 30 years ago while juggling a busy household and raising my children. I gradually decided to turn my passion into my profession.I love to make the most of every inch of space especially living in the city where space is limited. I relish the moment when it clicks & I understand what atmosphere my client is looking for. It gives me great pleasure to convert their dream living spaces into a reality.
